About Us

Established in 2007 by experienced journalist Nasir Abbas Babi, Jikan Babi Production has evolved from a regional electronic media presence in northern Nigeria to a nationally and internationally recognized broadcasting and media production company. Specializing in digital broadcasting services, the company has successfully provided affordable media production solutions for over 15 years. Notably, Jikan Babi Production has developed an online radio platform, JIKAN BABI RADIO, accessible at https://jikanbabi.com/radio and other radio hosting websites like Radio Garden. The company prides itself on creating audio and video content for ceremonies, awareness campaigns, education, and entertainment, fostering connections between clients and their audiences while also enhancing their online presence.
