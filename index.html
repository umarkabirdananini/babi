History

Established in 2007, Jikan Babi Production is essentially a broadcasting and media production company. The company was founded by a seasoned and well-accomplished journalist; Alhaji Nasir Abbas Babi. It was initially recognized through electronic media across the northern states of Nigeria and later boomerang to become a standard company with national and international recognition. From this position, we were prompt in handling efficient media production services and digital broadcasting for both local, national and international clients. 

Experience

Jikan Babi Production is proud to have been rendering effective digital broadcasting and media production services to an array of our clients at affordable prices. We have successfully established a friendly niche in the company where our clients get the best of our services. For more than 10 years, Jikan Babi Production has been relating with individuals and organizations in Nigeria and abroad to produce audio and video jingles for ceremonies, sensitization, awareness, mobilization, education and entertainment. We have also committed ourselves to the production of audios and videos to help our clients connect with their customers and promote their businesses.
Similarly, Jikan Babi Production helps in digitizing your online presence by making deliberate efforts to trigger your online presence targeting your specific customers. We create digital broadcast content for various media organizations.

Vision & Mission

With more than 30 experts as the production crew as of December 2021, we have made ebullient productions for more than 10,00 clients, including media organizations, public-sector organizations, private companies and appreciably more individuals in Sokoto and across the northern states in Nigeria. The company’s experienced production crew have expertise in mass communication, languages, translations, media production, journalism and of course in Information and Communication Technology. This has enabled us to deliver the best media production and digital broadcasting services in Nigeria. We aim to keep up with Nigeria’s evolving digital economy industry. The vision of the Jikan Babi Production is to serve our clients producing the desired results, thereby rendering them high-quality services that surpass their expectations, and thus help in building an egalitarian society for our clients and the general public.

Commitment to Clients

Jikan Babi Production is committed to the satisfaction of its clients. We comply with clients specifications and render the best service. We seek for clients feedback to improve on our general services. We also update our clients of all improvements and enhancements to our services to ensure that no compromise is made against our mission.
